The daily problem

Your school should not run on memory.

A martial arts studio is full of tiny signals: who missed class, who is almost ready to test, who needs a payment nudge, which trial family needs a call, and which student is quietly slipping away.

01

Spreadsheets hide the next action

A roster tab, an attendance tab, a payment note, and a rank list do not tell an owner what to do before tonight's first class.

02

Generic gym software misses the teaching model

A custom field for "belt" is not the same as real rank history, program-specific ladders, testing readiness, and instructor approval.

03

Legacy systems ask small schools to pay big-school prices

Koaryu is built for the owner-operator who needs serious structure without a heavy implementation or a bill that grows with every new student.
